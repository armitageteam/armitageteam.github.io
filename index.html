Etherkup is a side-project from the Bitkup—Armitage team to allow users to bet on individual matches secured by the Ethereum Blockchain

A Test-Drive

The solidity ecosystem is pretty wild, and we plan to get insights from a user centric test development.

We have an ambitious goal, but to accomplish it, we need to take smalls steps in order to guarantee the user security and well functioning of the platform built on top of the ethereum smart contracts.

Our final objective, should include a full decentralized bet platform, on where users can create and decide from where results should be fetched from, using external trusted oracles. By now, we aim to test how well a single game will be handled by the users, including the feedback given its taxes from Ethereum transaction fees, and also, check for relevant vulnerabilities that the bug bounty program can expose with the help of the community

This is a follow up of previous research and work from the Bitkup—Armitage team, that can enable the creation and innovation on multiple opportunities that we are currently involved, ranging from real state to political research.

Prior to the launch on the Mainnet, we are going to update fractions of the Alpha and possible features on the Ropsten Ethereum testnet, and as we feel comfortable and secure with a live production version of our platform with the help of the community feedback, you will be notified and invited to gamble with us.

This is currently an open source project that you can contribute and get involved with, and we are pleased to welcome people that aim to learn and create disruptive things. We are also currently developing private related projects that you can know more about in touch with us on our slack channel at Bitkup; feel free to come by and say hi.

Technical Features

This is a list of features that are in current development, and we grant the right to not launch some of these on the mainnet version.
Ropsten
  • Creation of a Match
  • Featuring a Match
  • Proposing of an Oracle
  • Voting on an Oracle
  • Rewards for proposing a winner Oracle
  • Rewards for voting on a winner Oracle
  • Check available pot for Match
  • Check available pot for a team
  • Make a bet on a Team
  • Withdraw Rewards
